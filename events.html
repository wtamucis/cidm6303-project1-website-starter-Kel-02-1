Tournaments and Events


Tournaments will be held regularly throughout the school year on our home campus, community centers, and online. Students will compete in skill-based and timed matches against fellow students from their home school as well as participants from other schools across the district. These tournaments are designed to encourage friendly competition, improve chess skills, and provide opportunities for students to represent their school in a supportive environment.


On tournament days, students are encouraged to bring a refillable water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the event. Healthy snacks such as fruit, granola bars, or crackers are also allowed, but please avoid messy or allergenic foods. Students may bring a personal chess set or clock if they wish, though tournament equipment will be provided. We recommend bringing a notebook and pen for taking notes or recording games. All personal items should be clearly labeled, and students are responsible for their belongings. Please remember to follow all school and event guidelines regarding permitted items.


Families and friends are welcome to attend and support our participants during these events. We kindly ask that all attendees follow the event guidelines and respect the players and organizers. Cell phones should be silenced or turned off during matches to avoid distractions and maintain a focused environment. A school photographer may be present to capture moments from the event and shared to the school's website or social media channels.


Check back here for updates on our upcoming chess events and tournaments!

For more information or to get involved, please contact us at chessclub@nimitzms.edu.
